GLOBAL MEET 2022

The 2022 edition of FS2022 is organised in collaboration with AMD – Akademie Mode & Design – Hochschule Fresenius – University of Applied Sciences. The AMD Akademie Mode & Design offers internationally certified programs in fashion. Promoting and supporting creative as well as business-oriented youth remains an essential strategic focus of the AMD Akademie Mode & Design for over 30 years of its dedicated educational activities. To enable students to create solutions for future sustainable design and fashion to become change makers for the creative industry.
